Quick Troubleshooting

SLASH COMMANDS

If commands aren't appearing, wait up to 1 hour for Discord to sync. Ensure the bot has "Use Application Commands" permission.

BOT PERMISSIONS

The bot requires Manage Roles and Ban Members to function. Ensure its role is placed ABOVE the users it manages.

EXP TRACKING

Confirm your division name matches the SEA Public Tracker exactly. Manual sync occurs every 10 minutes.

Full FAQ

How do I add OPSBOT to my division server? +

OPSBOT is currently in a controlled rollout. Access is granted to verified divisions. Contact us via Discord or Email to request an invite link. Once added, run /setup to launch the configuration wizard.

Does my division need to be official in SEA? +

No. Both official and unofficial divisions can use OPSBOT. However, the Division EXP module requires an official tracker entry to pull data. All other modules (Blacklist, Moderation, Events) work for everyone.

Why isn't the Blacklist auto-ban working? +

First, verify the module is active via /settings module toggle. Second, ensure OPSBOT has Ban Members permissions and that its role is higher than the users being banned in the Discord Hierarchy.

Can I disable modules I don't use? +

Yes. OPSBOT is completely modular. You can toggle Events, Moderation, Blacklist Sync, Weekly Analytics, and Officer Quotas individually to keep your server clean.

Who can access the bot's settings? +

By default, only server Administrators can configure the bot. You can delegate access to High Command or Officers by running /settings roles set to map your server roles to bot permission tiers.

How long does support take to respond? +

Response times for Discord tickets are usually under 4 hours. Email inquiries are typically processed within 24 hours.
