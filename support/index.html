Frequently Asked.

How do I get OPSBOT into my division server? ▶

OPSBOT is currently in closed beta, so in order to add it you have to submit a request via email or Discord. Once you have it in your server, run /setup and the wizard will walk you through the rest.

Does my division need to be official in SEA to use OPSBOT? ▶

No. Unofficial divisions can use all OPSBOT features except EXP tracking, which requires your division to appear in the SEA Division Tracker. During setup, you'll be asked whether your division is official or not.

Why isn't the blacklist auto-ban working? ▶

Make sure the blacklist module is enabled via /settings module toggle. Also verify OPSBOT has the Ban Members permission in your server. The blacklist is scanned every 30 minutes, so newly added entries may take up to 30 minutes to enforce.

Can I disable modules I don't need? ▶

Yes. Run /settings module toggle to enable or disable any module. Available modules include but are not limited to Events, Moderation, Blacklist Auto-Banning, Weekly Analytics, and Logging.

Who can use OPSBOT commands? ▶

Discord Administrators always have full access. Other members need to be assigned a role tier (High Command, Officer, or Trial Officer) via /settings roles set, with permissions configured per-tier.

How long does support take to respond? ▶

Response times are usually within 24 hours for both email and Discord.
